Welcome to the prototype APIjoe API, supported by the lovers of Blockchain News. The APIs found on APIjoe allow users to access article metadata like the article title, date published, time published, author of the article, and the link to the actual article for further exploration. APIjoe encourages innovators and researchers worldwide to query and analyze the articles for understanding the current climate and future trends in Blockchain.

The APIjoe APIs are intended to inspire the exploration and enhanced understanding of Blockchain technology and innovation systems. The database driving the API is regularly updated and integrates data quality control. We hope researchers and developers alike will explore the API to discover people and companies and to visualize trends and patterns across the Blockchain innovation landscape.

The API contains an HTTP endpoint that users can query to tell new stories such as:

  1. Which Blockchain companies are becoming popular? Which cryptocurrencies? In which technologies and companies is the public sentiment declining? Which have the most positive public sentiment? What are the common factors?
  2. What Blockchain technologies are most commonly used by other companies and governments in the last 5 years? Which companies have spiked in popularity over the recent weeks, months, years?
  3. Who are the most prolific authors in the last 3 decades? How has their article activity (number of articles, article topics, and publishing cadence) changed over time?
